How to make Peanut Butter Toast

By Dorian Cottle

When I was five I loved eating so much toast. It was my favorite. I would eat it all day every day.

Getting Started

  • Peanut Butter
  • Bread
  • Topping (I prefer honey or jelly)
  • Milk (not required but definitely helps wash it down)

Directions

  1. Put bread in toaster until it pops up.
  2. Spread as much peanut butter as you want on the bread with a knife.
  3. Top it off with some delicious topping.
  4. I like to dip the toast in milk but other people think that's wierd so if that's not for you don't do it.
